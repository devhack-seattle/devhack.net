what is /dev/hack?

/dev/hack is a hackerspace in seattle's university district, where folks come together to tinker, learn, share ideas, and find community.

/dev/hack also:

  • peers with local organizations and runs as a volunteer-run nonprofit
  • hosts space infrastructure and member projects that you can read more about
  • empowers you to contribute towards anything you see -- it's a do-ocracy
